Experiencing issues? Here are some common ones:

#1 Reward Not Found.

This issue usually occurs if the reward was created recently or you accidently entered the wrong pin. Usually, this can be fixed by reloading the page and entering the pin in again with care.

#2 Reward Recently Deleted.

This issue usually occurs if the reward creator's plan expired or we deleted it because it didn't meet our requirements, etc. and you should try contacting the creator.
